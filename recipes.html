Barbeque chicken


Prep time: 20 minutes
Cook time: 30 minutes
Ingredients
  1. 2 quarts water
  2. I1 cup sugar
  3. 1sp salt
  4. 1/2 cup homemade bbq sauce
  5. 3 chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
  6. 1 glove of garlic
  7. 1/2 tbsp of onion powder
  8. 1 chile (red)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degress.
  2. In a bowl mix all ingredients instead of chicken.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and place in the over. Cover.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes or until the juices run clear
  6. Pour over the sauce over the chicken.
  7. Bake for another 5 minutes or until sauce thickens over the chicken.
  8. Remove from the oven and serve with vegetables.

simple salad


Prep time: 5 minute
Cook time: 0
Ingredients
  1. 1 cup of lettuce
  2. 1 cup of baby spinach
  3. 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes
  4. 1 cucumber
  5. 2 radishes
  6. 1 carrot (peeled)
  7. 2 tbsp of your favorite dressing

Instructions:

  1. On a cutting board, slice the radishes and carrot
  2. In a large bowl mix all ingredients.
  3. Toss with a large spoon and fork.
  4. Serve immediately or place in refrigerator.

Chocolate pudding


Prep time: 8 minutes
Cook time: 20 minutes
Ingredients
  1. 1 cup of sugar
  2. 1 tsp of cinnamon
  3. 3 tbsp of cocoa chips, unsweetened
  4. 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  5. pinch of salt
  6. 3 cups of milk
  7. 2 tbsp of butter
  8. 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, stir together sugar, cocoa, cornstarch and salt.
  2. Put saucepan on the stove and change the setting to medium-high heat.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil and cook until mixture thickens, stirring constantly.
  4. Remove saucepan to a cool element and stir in butter and vanilla extract. Do NOT melt butter beforehand.
  5. Let cool and serve warm.
  6. Chill in refrigerator after done.
